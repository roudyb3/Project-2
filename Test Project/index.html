Top 5 states/bottom 5 states



These bar charts show the top five and bottom five states ranked according to what percentage of their adult population is fully vaccinated, using the most recent data available at the time of the charts' creation.


Vaccination Rate by State



This radar chart demonstrates the gap between the total number of vaccines distributed and the total number of adults who are fully vaccinated in the four most populous states, plus Illinois.

Scatterplot


This scatter plot takes the poverty rate, unemployment rate, and median household income of each of the 50 states - plus Washington DC - prior to the pandemic and compares them against the full vaccionation rates of each state, as well as the percentage of each state's population that has taken additional shots.
